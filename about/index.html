Welcome to Thurston Developments Ltd.

We’re a locally owned business, servicing the Bay of Plently and Waikato regions. We provide Quality Drainage services, Earthworks and Landscaping. Our work is carried out by Jordan Thurston, a fully qualified and certified Drainlayer with the New Zealand Plumbing Gasfitting and Drainlaying Board.

Jordan has worked extensively in both the commercial and residential drainage and earthworks industries for the past 10 years. We aim to provide maximum value for money without jeopardizing quality. Thurston Developments works closely with all its clients to ensure complete satisfaction and we pride ourselves on meeting and exceeding the expectations of our valued customers.

At Thurston Developments, we are only satisfied when you are!
